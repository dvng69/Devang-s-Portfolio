About Me

I am a 3D Animator specializing in game animation and stylized character motion. I primarily work in Autodesk Maya, creating expressive performances, dynamic combat animations, and cinematic sequences.

My focus is on bringing characters to life through strong poses, clear storytelling, and polished movement designed for engaging gameplay and visual impact.

I studied animation and multimedia where I developed strong fundamentals in character performance, body mechanics and cinematic storytelling.

Currently I work as a 3D Animator creating stylized animations and cinematic motion for digital experiences and interactive media.
